Corsair application

Corsair is a GPS for pedestrians. It allows you to discover places around you and take you there. A new way of guidance has been developed by using the smartphone vibrate to indicate the direction to follow. This solution is particularly useful for people with visual impairments.

Features

  • Voice Search
  • Finding Places
  • Around me : list the nearby places by category
  • Calculation of the pedestrian route
  • Route using public transport (if available)
  • Voice Guidance
  • Multi-task (works when the device is locked)
  • Guidance by vibration (Press the compass and orient your phone, it vibrates when you point it in the right direction)
  • Settings: disable voice synthesis, disable the vibrator

Additional features for the blind:

  • Suitable position and buttons size
  • Headsets Button to repeat the speech
  • Where am I: give the address where you are


All features are available for free and are designed to simplify your life.
